Game Rules

Use arrow keys (up, down, left, right) or other designated keys to change the direction of the snake's movement.

There will be randomly placed candy items on the game board. The snake must move over the candy to eat it and gain points.

After eating food, the snake grows longer by adding a segment to its tail. The longer the snake gets, the more challenging it becomes to navigate without colliding with the walls or its own tail.

The game ends if the snake collides with any of the following:
Walls: If the snake hits the boundary walls of the game board.
Self: If the snake's head collides with any part of its own body.
